Clustering MBCC 2023


Perhaps this is the job that I have enjoyed the most because I brought together my two passions, craft beer and data, a clustering of the 786 different beers (and other drinks) of the MBCC 2023 made by Mikkeller, the famous Danish brewery.
I have collected the data from Untappd where all the data of the beers of the 4 sessions are found, with Power BI I have consolidated everything in a single csv file.
This clustering begins with an exploratory analysis of the data, then we apply the K-means algorithm with the elbow method and the silhouettes to determine how many clusters are optimal for this analysis.
Available is the csv with the raw data, all the Python code and the csv with the clustering result.
